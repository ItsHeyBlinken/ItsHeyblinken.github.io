Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our web design services

How long does it take to build a custom website?

Most custom websites take 2-4 weeks to complete, depending on complexity and content readiness. Template-based sites can be delivered in 1-2 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What's included in your web design packages?

All packages include responsive design, SEO optimization, contact forms, social media integration, and basic analytics setup. Premium packages also include e-commerce functionality, advanced SEO, and ongoing maintenance.

Do you provide ongoing website maintenance?

Yes! We offer monthly and yearly maintenance packages that include security updates, content updates, performance monitoring, and technical support. This ensures your website stays secure and up-to-date.

Can you help with SEO and getting found on Google?

Absolutely! All our websites include basic SEO optimization with proper meta tags, structured data, and fast loading speeds. We also offer advanced SEO services including keyword research, content optimization, and local SEO setup.

What if I need changes after the website is complete?

We include 2 rounds of revisions in all packages, and offer unlimited minor tweaks for 30 days after launch. For ongoing changes, we provide affordable hourly rates or maintenance packages to keep your site updated.
