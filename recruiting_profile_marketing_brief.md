# Marketing Brief: Athlete Recruiting Profile Websites

**Document Version:** 1.0
**Date:** July 14, 2026
**Author:** Manus AI

## 1. Executive Summary
This marketing brief outlines the strategy for launching and promoting a new service offering by BytesByBlinken LLC: professional athlete recruiting profile websites. This service aims to provide student-athletes with a compelling online presence to showcase their athletic and academic achievements to college coaches and recruiters. Leveraging a data-driven template, the service offers a cost-effective, high-impact solution with ongoing maintenance and support.

## 2. Service Offering: "Collegiate Bound Profiles"
BytesByBlinken LLC will offer custom-designed, mobile-responsive athlete recruiting profile websites. Each profile will feature comprehensive athletic and academic information, highlight videos, downloadable recruitment packets, and direct contact options for coaches. The service includes domain registration, secure hosting, and regular content updates.

## 3. Target Audience

### 3.1. Primary Audience: Student-Athletes & Parents
*   **Demographics**: High school student-athletes (ages 14-18) and their parents/guardians, primarily in competitive sports (e.g., softball, baseball, basketball, soccer, volleyball) who aspire to play at the collegiate level.
*   **Needs**: A professional, easy-to-share platform to stand out in the competitive college recruiting landscape. Parents seek a convenient, reliable, and effective way to market their child to college programs.
*   **Pain Points**: Difficulty in getting noticed by college coaches, outdated or unprofessional recruiting materials, lack of technical expertise to build and maintain a website, time constraints.

### 3.2. Secondary Audience: High School & Club Coaches
*   **Needs**: A streamlined way to present their athletes to college recruiters, a professional resource to share with college programs, and a tool to enhance their program's reputation.
*   **Pain Points**: Time-consuming process of compiling individual athlete profiles, inconsistent presentation of athlete information, limited resources for web development.

## 4. Unique Selling Proposition (USP)
**"Professional, hassle-free online recruiting profiles that make athletes shine. We handle the tech, you focus on the game."**

Our service provides student-athletes with a dedicated, dynamic web presence that is always up-to-date, easy to share, and optimized for college coaches.
