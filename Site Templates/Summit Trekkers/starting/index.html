About Us

More Than Just a Hiking Club

Summit Trekkers is a community of passionate outdoor enthusiasts who share a love for nature, adventure, and connection.

Founded in 2018, Summit Trekkers brings together people of all ages and experience levels who share a passion for exploring the great outdoors. Our club organizes regular hiking trips, from gentle nature walks to challenging mountain ascents, creating opportunities for members to challenge themselves, discover beautiful landscapes, and forge meaningful connections.

What makes Summit Trekkers special is our emphasis on community. Every adventure concludes with a social gathering at a local restaurant or café, where members can refuel, share stories, and strengthen friendships in a relaxed setting.

Our Adventures

Explore the Great Outdoors

From gentle nature walks to challenging mountain ascents, we offer adventures for every experience level.

Beginner

Forest Trails

Gentle walks through scenic woodland paths, perfect for beginners and families. These 2-3 hour hikes feature minimal elevation gain and plenty of natural beauty.

2-3 hours 100-200m elevation
Intermediate

Mountain Paths

Moderately challenging hikes with rewarding views. These 4-5 hour adventures include significant elevation gain and varied terrain, suitable for those with some hiking experience.

4-5 hours 400-600m elevation
Advanced

Summit Challenges

Challenging full-day expeditions to reach breathtaking summits. These adventures require good fitness levels and previous hiking experience, but offer unforgettable views and a sense of achievement.

6-8 hours 800-1200m elevation
