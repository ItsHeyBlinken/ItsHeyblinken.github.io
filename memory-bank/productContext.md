# Product context

## Audience

Small businesses that need a web presence but do not know where to start; messaging emphasizes clarity, templates, custom work, and transparent next steps.

## Positioning

- Agency-style presentation (BytesByBlinken Media) with template catalog and pricing tiers.
- Contact flow: **email first** → response SLA messaging → call for details when appropriate.

## Key surfaces

| Page | Role |
|------|------|
| Home | Value proposition, trust, CTAs, FAQ-style content as designed |
| Templates | Filterable or browsable template gallery; links to demo sites under `Site Templates/` |
| Pricing | Packages and pricing narrative |

## Content and compliance

- Google Analytics via gtag (ID present in HTML).
- `google-site-verification` meta where configured.
