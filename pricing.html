Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?

Most projects are completed within 1-2 weeks from receiving all required content and approvals. Complex projects may take longer.

Do I need to provide my own hosting?

No, all our packages include hosting setup and configuration, so you don't have to worry about technical details.

What happens after my maintenance plan expires?

You can renew your maintenance plan or switch to a different one. If you choose not to renew, your website will still function, but won't receive updates or support.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade your website or maintenance plan at any time. We'll apply a prorated credit for any unused portion of your current plan.
