FAQ

Pricing & engagement questions, answered.

Quick answers about timelines, hosting, scope changes, and ongoing maintenance.

How fast does a website plan go live?

Most brochure-style websites ship in two to four weeks once assets and approvals keep pace. Commerce and custom integrations follow a milestone roadmap you approve in writing—so dates and deliverables stay predictable.

Do I need my own hosting?

Most small-business clients choose our managed hosting, domain, and SSL bundle so launch week is not a DNS scavenger hunt. Already standardized on enterprise hosting? We integrate with your policy—what's in-scope vs. out-of-scope is documented up front.

What if I pause Site Care?

Your website or storefront stays online, but you lose proactive updates, scheduled backups, and priority fixes. We recommend Care on demand at minimum so performance and security drift don't compound quietly.

Can we change scope after kickoff?

Yes—scope changes flow through a short change request with revised timeline and investment before work proceeds. Moving between plans mid-flight? We reconcile what's already activated, then quote only the delta—no mystery math.

Do you offer payment plans for small businesses?

Yes. Website plans typically split into a deposit at kickoff and balance at launch, with longer schedules available on Signature and Commerce engagements. Site Care subscriptions bill monthly or yearly. Everything is documented in your statement of work before we start.

What's included with SEO on every plan?

Every website plan ships with on-page SEO foundations—proper headings, meta tags, structured data, clean URLs, fast loading, and accessible markup. Signature adds deeper on-page SEO and structured data planning. Ongoing keyword research and content optimization can be scoped into Site Care or a custom engagement; we never sell ranking guarantees.
