Frequently asked questions

How fast does a plan go live?

Most brochure-style activations ship in two to four weeks once assets and approvals keep pace. Commerce and custom integrations follow a milestone roadmap you approve in writing—so dates and deliverables stay predictable.

Do I need my own hosting?

Most clients choose our managed hosting, domain, and SSL bundle so launch week is not a DNS scavenger hunt. Already standardized on enterprise hosting? We integrate with your policy—what is in-scope vs. out-of-scope is documented up front.

What if I pause Site Care?

Your storefront or site stays online, but you lose proactive updates, scheduled backups, and priority fixes. We recommend Care on demand at minimum so performance and security drift do not compound quietly.

Can we change scope after kickoff?

Yes—scope changes flow through a short change request with revised timeline and investment before work proceeds. Moving between plans mid-flight? We reconcile what is already activated, then quote only the delta—no mystery math.
